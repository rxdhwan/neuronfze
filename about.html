Who We Are

Established in 2022. Headquartered in Dubai.

Our Story

Previously operated under the name 'Neuron International DMCC', Neuron International FZE is a specialized trading company located at Jebel Ali Freezone. Since our inception in 2022, we have dedicated ourselves to becoming the primary supply chain partner for the rapidly growing Lab Grown Diamond (LGD) manufacturing sector.

We understand the precision required in Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). That is why we do not just trade; we curate. We import only the highest standard of machinery parts—from Mass Flow Controllers to Quartz Tubes—sourcing directly from technological hubs in Germany, Japan, Netherlands, China, France and the USA.

Our Mission

To bridge the technological gap between global innovation and local manufacturing in the UAE and India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in high-vacuum and CVD machinery procurement in the Middle East.
