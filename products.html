Alicat Mass Flow Controllers

Designed for Diamonds. Alicat's diamond mass flow controllers provide precise gas balancing for stable growth rates. They automatically adjust for temperature and pressure changes during ionization.

Technical Specifications

  • Multi-gas support: Hydrogen, CO2, Methane, Argon, Oxygen
  • Control Range: 0.01-100% of full scale
  • Response Time: 30 ms
  • Accuracy: As good as ±0.5% of reading
  • Repeatability: ±(0.1% of reading + 0.02% of full scale)

Alicat Pressure Controllers

Alicat's diamond pressure controllers provide stable control of system pressure during CVD processes. Devices are equipped with a fast-acting proportional control valve which rapidly responds to maintain stable pressure.

Technical Specifications

  • Control Range: 0.5-100% of full scale
  • Response Time: 30 ms
  • Leak Integrity: 10e-9 atm-cc/sec (Exceptional leak integrity)
  • Repeatability: ±0.08% of full scale
  • Accuracy: As good as ±0.125% of full scale

High-Purity CVD Grade Quartz

High-purity quartz tubes capable of withstanding extreme thermal and plasma conditions inside CVD reactors. They offer transparency, excellent thermal shock resistance, and chemical inertness.

Applications in CVD Diamond Growth

  • Used as the primary chamber liner for microwave CVD reactors
  • Improves diamond purity by minimizing contamination
  • Provides excellent transparency for optical monitoring of plasma
  • Offers high resistance to thermal shock during rapid heating/cooling

Molybdenum Disc & Ring

Engineered molybdenum components designed for high-temperature CVD applications. They exhibit excellent thermal conductivity, low expansion, and strong resistance to plasma erosion.

Applications

  • Substrate holders for consistent thermal distribution
  • Support components for diamond growth plates
  • Reduces thermal deformation and contamination during long reaction cycles
